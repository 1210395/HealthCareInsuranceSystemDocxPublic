# What Is This System?

## Overview

The Healthcare Insurance Management System is a complete digital platform that connects everyone involved in healthcare insurance: the insurance company, doctors, pharmacists, laboratory staff, radiology staff, and patients. Instead of paper forms, phone calls, and long waiting times, this system lets everyone do their work online, in real time, from anywhere.

Think of it as a shared workspace where insurance claims, prescriptions, lab tests, and payments all flow smoothly from one person to the next, with everyone able to see exactly where things stand at any moment.


## Who Uses It?

**Insurance Company Staff**
- **Insurance Managers** run the overall system. They set up policies, manage users, and oversee everything.
- **Medical Administrators** review whether a medical claim makes sense from a healthcare perspective.
- **Coordination Administrators** review the financial side of claims and process payments.

**Healthcare Providers**
- **Doctors** treat patients, submit claims, write prescriptions, and order lab or radiology tests.
- **Pharmacists** receive prescriptions, verify them, and dispense medicine to patients.
- **Lab Technicians** receive lab test requests, process the tests, and upload results.
- **Radiologists** receive imaging requests (such as X-rays or MRIs), perform the imaging, and upload results.

**Patients**
- **Insurance Clients** (patients) use the system to track their claims, view prescriptions and test results, manage family members on their plan, and communicate with the insurance company.


## What Problems Does It Solve?

| Old Way (Paper-Based) | New Way (This System) |
|---|---|
| Claims submitted on paper forms | Claims submitted instantly online |
| Weeks of waiting with no updates | Real-time tracking of every claim |
| Phone calls to check claim status | Status visible in the app at any time |
| Paper prescriptions that can be lost | Digital prescriptions sent directly to pharmacies |
| Manual calculation of coverage amounts | Automatic calculation based on policy rules |
| No way to verify patient identity quickly | Instant verification using QR codes on mobile |
| Records stored in filing cabinets | All records stored securely in a digital system |
| Difficult to manage family members | Family members managed easily in one place |
| One language only | Available in both Arabic and English |
| One currency only | Supports Israeli Shekel, Jordanian Dinar, and US Dollar |


## Key Benefits

**Faster Claims Processing**
Claims that used to take weeks can now be reviewed and processed in days or even hours. Every step happens digitally, with no paper to get lost in transit.

**Real-Time Tracking**
Patients, doctors, and insurance staff can all see the current status of any claim or request. No more calling the insurance company to ask "Where is my claim?"

**Mobile Access for Patients**
Patients have a mobile app where they can view their medical history, show their insurance identity using a QR code at the doctor's office, manage their family members, and receive notifications about their claims.

**Secure Data**
All personal and medical information is protected with strong security measures. Each person can only see the information that is relevant to their role.

**Automatic Coverage Calculation**
When a claim is submitted, the system automatically calculates how much the insurance covers and how much the patient needs to pay, based on the policy rules. No manual math, no errors.

**Multi-Language Support**
The system works in both Arabic and English, making it accessible to all users in the region.

**Multi-Currency Support**
The system handles transactions in Israeli Shekels (ILS), Jordanian Dinars (JOD), and US Dollars (USD), with daily exchange rate updates.

## What Makes This System Special

**Multi-Stage Review Process**
Every claim goes through at least two levels of review: first a medical review (does this claim make medical sense?) and then a financial review (does this match the patient's policy?). This ensures accuracy and fairness.

**Automatic Coverage Calculation**
The system knows the patient's insurance policy and automatically calculates the exact amounts. It considers general policy rules, category-specific rules (for example, different rates for dental versus lab work), and even service-specific rules (for example, a specific rate for blood tests). The most specific rule always applies.

**Real-Time Chat Support**
Patients can communicate directly with insurance administrators through a built-in chat feature. Messages are private and secure.

**Organization Support**
The system supports hospitals and private clinics as organizations. Each organization can have its own staff, its own policies, and its own workflow, all within the same system.

**Family Coverage**
A single insurance plan can cover an entire family. The primary account holder can manage their spouse, children, parents, and siblings, all from one account.

**No Data Is Ever Lost**
When something is removed from the system, it is hidden from view but never permanently deleted. This ensures a complete history is always available for accountability and auditing purposes.
